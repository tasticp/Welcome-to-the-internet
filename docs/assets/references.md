## üìù Citation Guidelines

### **Academic Standards**

**APA Format:**
```text
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of work. Publisher.
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of article. *Journal Title, volume*(issue), pages.
```

**MLA Format:**
```text
Author. Title. Publisher, Year.
Author. "Title of Article." *Journal Title*, vol. #, no. #, Year, pp. #-#.
```

### **Digital Sources**

**Online Articles:**
```text
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). *Title of article*. Website Name. URL
```

**Social Media:**
```text
Author [@username]. (Year, Month Day). *Content of post up to 20 words* [Post]. Social Media Platform. URL
```
